Transitions guide
The connectors that make you sound fluent — master these first
When
To shift perspective or introduce a new angle on the same topic — mid-conversation, without breaking flow.
طالما إحنا من ناحية الشهرة، خلينا نتكلم عن الأصحاب.
tala ma ihna min nahyat ash-shuhra, khalina nitkallam 3an al-ashab.
Since we're on fame, let's talk about friends.
When
When landing on a conclusion. "Alu hwa" closes a reasoning chain; "alu ino" introduces what it means.
ألو هو الزول دا ما كان مستعد للشهرة.
alu hwa az-zool da ma kan musta3id lil-shuhra.
So that's it — that person wasn't ready for fame.
